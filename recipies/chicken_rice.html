Classic Chicken and Rice Feast

Description

This recipe is a tried and true favourite that provides a balanced combination of lean protein and carbohydrates, making it suitable for cats of all ages and dietary requirements.

To prepare this delicious meal, you will need boneless chicken, cooked rice, and a few additional ingredients to enhance the flavour and nutritional value. We'll guide you through the step-by-step process, ensuring your cat enjoys a scrumptious homemade meal that is free from preservatives and artificial additives.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Chicken Broth
  • Rice
  • Carrots
  • Peas

Instructions

  1. Cook the chicken breast thoroughly and shred it into small, bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the cooked rice, chicken broth, carrots, and peas.
  3. Add the shredded chicken to the rice mixture and mix well.
  4. Serve the meal in your cat's dish and ensure it has cooled completely to room temperature before feeding
